Ingredients:
- 100g noodles
- 200g sauce of choice
- breeze of tymian
- teespoon of oregano
- pepper and salt to your liking
- parmesan

Directions:
1. cook noodles in salt water for ~10 minutes
2. heat up sauce for 4/5 minutes
3. add tymian and oregano
4. add pepper and salt
5. garnish with parmesan
